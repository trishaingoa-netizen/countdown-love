Until I See You

I was just thinking about November… about Hyderabad… about how I didn’t even realise in those 4 days that something in me had already started changing.
I remember the way you looked at me… like you already knew what you felt. And I didn’t know how to hold something that certain back then.
You were so sure about us. And I wasn’t… not because I didn’t feel it, but because I didn’t understand how something could be that real, that quickly.
I think a part of me was scared… because if what you felt was true, then it meant I was about to feel it too.
I think this is the part where everything feels quiet… and honest. And if I’m being honest— somewhere along the way, you stopped being just someone I met and became someone I can’t imagine my days without.
And the strangest part is… it didn’t happen all at once. It was in the small things. The way you stayed. The way you cared. The way you chose me, every day.
It took me time… but somewhere between then and now, I didn’t just understand your love… I fell into it.
And now… I don’t question it anymore. I don’t overthink it. I just feel it… fully.
And I keep going back to January… me showing up in Hyderabad… not just to surprise you, but because something in me already knew— you mattered more than I was ready to admit.
Since then… it’s been us, constantly. Calls, texts, silence, everything in between. And in all of that, you showed me a kind of love I didn’t know existed.
And somewhere along the way… without even realising when it happened… I stopped holding back. I stopped questioning. I just… drowned in it.
Okay… now I’m not calm anymore. Now it’s real.
And now I’m here. Not confused. Not unsure. Not afraid of how deep this goes. Just yours… in a way that feels easy, real, and unconditional.
