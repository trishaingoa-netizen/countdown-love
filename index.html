Until I See You

I don’t think you realise this… but tonight doesn’t feel like any other night to me.
I was just thinking about November… about Hyderabad… about how something in me had already started changing.
I remember the way you looked at me… like you already knew what you felt.
You were so sure about us. And I wasn’t… not because I didn’t feel it.
I think a part of me was scared… because it meant I was about to feel it too.
Somewhere along the way… you stopped being just someone I met… and became someone I can’t imagine my days without.
It was in the small things. The way you stayed. The way you chose me, every day.
I didn’t just understand your love… I fell into it.
Now I don’t question it anymore. I just feel it… fully.
January… me showing up… because something in me already knew you mattered.
You showed me a kind of love I didn’t know existed.
I stopped holding back. I just… drowned in it.
Okay… now it’s real.
And now I’m here. Just yours… ❤️
